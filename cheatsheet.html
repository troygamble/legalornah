üìÇ Uploading Files for LLM Assistance

If you need an AI assistant to understand your project, upload these core files:

  • _config.yml - Jekyll site settings and plugin configurations.
  • generate_content.py - The AI script that generates legal Q&A content.
  • _questions/ - The folder containing AI-generated Markdown files.
  • _layouts/question.html - The Jekyll layout for displaying Q&A pages.
  • index.md - The homepage of your Jekyll site.
  • questions.md - The page that lists all questions.
  • _site/ (optional) - The generated output after running Jekyll.
  • requirements.txt - The dependencies needed for your Python script.

Uploading these will give the AI assistant a clear understanding of your project setup.
